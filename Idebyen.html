Idébyen is an app that aims to promote development and innovation in the Danish villages. In the app, you experience a map on which you as a user can place an idea. It is only the imagination that sets the limits of what ideas one can share. It could be anything from the construction of a new path, a flea market at the local common area or maybe a football school? Everything is relevant and no ideas should be hidden away. The city of ideas is about development, change and improvement in your particular village. Once an idea is shared, you and everyone else in town can indicate whether they liked the idea and whether they are willing to help volunteer on the idea. Last but not least, you have the opportunity to support the idea financially. Villages are made up of enthusiasts and many have really good suggestions for changes, improvements, etc. Unfortunately, almost as many are wasted as people either do not have the time or information on where and how to share their vision. We want to make the process from idea to action shorter and more manageable for the citizen. We want to make it attractive for potential citizens to move to a village where their voice is heard. We hope that your city also wanted to use the City of Ideas as a tool in connection with the development and improvement of your city. If this is the case and your city is still not registered in the city of ideas, then you are very welcome to contact us on some of the boards from your city. You can contact us via. Facebook.
