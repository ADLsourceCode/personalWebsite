SHOPEE Store e-Commerce App is for both Android and IOS . The App is clean, well structured with smooth animation. Creative and modern design with great UI/UX. The App Serves customers to explore and buy various good online.

Key Features:

  • Email and Phone Authentication
  • Social Login for Authentication (Google, Facebook)
  • Registration of New user
  • Product Categories
  • Category wise Product Listing
  • Products with Attribute Customisation
  • Cart and Checkout Workflow
  • Multiple Payment Gateway
  • Order and Payment History
  • User Wallet to add and withdraw money
  • Customisable Product Details
  • Review and Rating System 
  • Push and In-app notifications
  • Product Search And Recommendations
  • Wishlist and Favourite
  • Language Settings
  • Legal and Privacy Page
  • User Profile
  • Order Tracking System
  • Help & FAQs
